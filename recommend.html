We’re constantly on the lookout for great films to review. If you have a suggestion, send it our way. We cover all kinds of independent films, from documentary to narrative, from foreign to domestic. In order for films to be eligible for review, they must be available on DVD.

If you’d like us to recommend your own film and it’s not widely available, it’s best to mail us a physical copy. If we like it enough to share it with the world, we’ll feature it on the Indie Film Minute and spread the word.

If you’re an established writer who loves films, you’re welcome to submit a review for our consideration. Please note that Indie Film Minute will not consider reviews that have been previously published online in any form, including on social media and private blogs. All reviews published by the Indie Film Minute in any format will be the exclusive property of the Indie Film Minute brand. We do not currently assign a byline to audio reviews. If we decide to use at least 80% of your material in an Indie Film Minute review, we will pay you fifty dollars for ownership of the review. In order to ensure a consistency in our style, we reserve the right to make edits to accepted content.

To be used, and therefore paid in association, the submission must be less than 240 words long. The film title should be mentioned both early and late in the review. Each of our reviews is unique. The only way to get a feel for our style and the kind of content we publish is to [[visit our archives]] [[link to]] and listen to past reviews.
