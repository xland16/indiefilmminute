Sarah Crossland

Executive Director

Sarah Crossland is the executive director of and the newest addition to the Indie Film Minute. With degrees in storytelling and creative writing from the University of Virginia and the University of Wisconsin at Madison, respectively, she brings a creative approach to marketing and fan and subscriber engagement. An avid movie-lover, Sarah has seen every Academy Award-Winning Best Picture and every film on the American Film Institute’s “100 Years…100 Movie: Greatest American Films of All Time” list. Her favorite movie of all time is Victor Eríce’s 1973 magical realist film El espíritu de la colmena. A widely published poet, Sarah is currently working on a hybrid poetry/screenplay manuscript called 62 Short Films on Creation, which reimagines 62 different creation myths from around the world using the Surrealist trope of the “unfilmable film.” When Sarah’s not streaming her favorite Criterions on HuluPlus, you can find her catching the latest indie flick at her beloved Cinema Arts Theatre in Northern Virginia. For indie film viewing at home, she recommends popcorn with black truffle oil and Maldon flaked sea salt.
