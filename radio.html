Whether it’s the tearjerkers or the comedies that make you laugh, movies bring us all together. Here at the Indie Film Minute, we love to showcase the very best independent films we can find.

There are lots of sources for finding information on the big Hollywood films, but for those who want richer content, the Indie Film Minute is the best resource for discovering the best lesser-known films. Our short radio feature recommends three independent movies each week, and across the board our listeners agree: they want to hear the Indie Film Minute on all their favorite radio stations.

Indie Film Minute is unique and accessible. Since we only feature films available for rent, our recommendations are timeless, and the movies we review can be accessed by anyone, anywhere. With three years on the air and counting, the Indie Film Minute is a proven method to reach and engage your listeners in a new and exciting way.

We deliver three shows per week via FTP. Shows for the following week are posted by end of the business day on Thursdays the week before intended broadcast. Our shows are ninety seconds in length with a thirty second commercial. Our general plan is barter, but we’d also be happy to consider a direct subscription if that is your preference.
